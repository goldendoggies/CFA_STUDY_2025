Discount for Lack of Marketability (DLOM)

Discount for Lack of Marketability (DLOM)

Used to discount the valuation

Total Discount = [1 – (1 – DLOC)×(1 – DLOM)]

Discount for Lack of Control (DLOC)

Discount for Lack of Control (DLOC)

Used to discount the valuation

DLOC = 1 – [1/(1 + Control premium)]

Total Discount = [1 – (1 – DLOC)×(1 – DLOM)]

Income Based Method

Income Based Method of Valuing Private Equity

Several steps depending on what information is provided.

  1. Calculate working capital (WC * Required return for WC)
  2. Fixed Assets (fixed assets * RR for fixed assets)
  3. Excess Earnings = Normalized Excess Earnings - WC - Fixed Assets
  4. Value of Intangible Assets = Excess Earnings * 1 + growth of residual earnings/ required return of intangibles - growth rate of residual earnings
