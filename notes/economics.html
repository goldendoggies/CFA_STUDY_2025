Triangular Arbitrage

Triangular Arbitrage

Arbitrage opportunity if:

  • Dealer Bid > Internbank Offer
  • Dealer Offer < Interbank Bid

If these two conditions hold, there will NOT be an arbitrage opportunity:

  • Dealer Bid < Internbank Offer
  • Dealer Offer > Interbank Bid
  1. Compare dealer bid/offer with interbank bid/offer
  2. observe bid and offer on two pairs, find implied cross rate
    • May need to invert the bid and offer to make things comparable
    • Then multiply bids and offers to find implied cross rate

Mark to market

Mark to market of Forward currency rates

  1. Enter forward into offsetting position
  2. Add or subtract forward points appropriately to extrage rate
  3. subtract forward rate at initiation from forward offsetting rate, then multiply by notional amount
  4. Take result from forward mark to market and discount back to initiation using appropriate currency's discount rate
