Process

Current Process:

  • Identify at random a single reading. You will see a randomized topic selected from a list of all readings available.
  • Watch Mark Meldrum's video on the topic, in full.
  • Review CFA LOS via ecosystem
  • Complete a number of Mark Meldrum or CFA ecosystem question bank items
  • Identify common themes and mark notes on this site. Add 'flash cards' and/or tools to assist in future questions on the topic
  • Rinse and repeat

It is important to me to try to cover multiple topics quickly, while trying to grasp the core ideas of each reading before moving on. the more topics covered in one sitting, with good understanding and good practice is key to learning. Also making multiple passes over the same material and practicing consistently on harder topics is key.

After completing the above process for a reading or several readings in the same sitting, mark which topics you have covered with a ranking of the understanding of the material, along with the current date. If you rank a reading as 'Bad' review again soon. In my example, I try to review it four days later at the latest.
